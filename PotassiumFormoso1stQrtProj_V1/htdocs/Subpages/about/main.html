About me

Personally, I have played over 100 hours worth of civ 5, not that much considering some people go over 5 000 when playing civ, but still a lot of time and commitment towards it. I want to use it in some way to convince people to play games like civ (ie. Humankind, Age of Empires, etc.) because they are fun and quite addicting in a sense.

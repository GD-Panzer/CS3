Capital Settling

Your capital is the most important city in Civ 5. It generally has the most buildings out of every city. The problem is, where to optimally place it? (Conditions: 1 luxury resource and 1 strateigic resource near spawn point + river next to hill) Personally, I would settle the capital near the river on top of a hill. This is because being on a hill gives you a bonus to production in the early game, so much further expansion can be made, and a river has lots of unique structures that help in the growth and prodiction of that city.

Another helpful thing to note is that being on a hill + next to a river gives you lots of defensive bonuses against attackers. A hill gives +25% defensive bonus to the city, while the river gives the attackers a 20% debuff from attacking across a river
